Why We Are Asking

The EU Corporate Sustainability Reporting Directive (CSRD) and the European Sustainability Reporting Standards (ESRS) require The Corporate to report on the impacts of its full value chain from 2026. We cannot meet that obligation without data from our suppliers.

72% of our carbon footprint sits in the value chain — in the products, materials, and services our Tier 1 suppliers provide. Our 2045 Net-Zero target depends on visibility into that 72%. This assessment provides it.

This is a shared responsibility. Your data shapes the joint decarbonisation plans that follow, and strong sustainability performance is recognised in our procurement decisions. We are building a resilient supply chain together — one supplier at a time.
